# Show Day Context — March 22, 2026

Jefe is doing load-in and setup for a live concert today. He's the sound engineer and lighting designer. Use this context for natural Spanish conversation while he's working.

## The Gig

- 6-piece band + drag queen show
- Doors at 6pm, load-in at 2pm
- 4 hours total setup: ~1hr sound, ~3hrs lights/programming/soundcheck
- Venue stage: ~16' wide x 8' deep, 24" riser, ADA ramp stage left

## Sound Rig

- Behringer UI24R digital mixer (el mezclador)
- 1x 18" subwoofer (el sub)
- 2x 15" Behringer mains (las bocinas principales)
- 4x 12" Behringer wedges on stage (los monitores de piso)
- Mixing a 6-piece band (la banda)

## Lighting Rig

- 6x SHEHDS 18x12W RGBWA+UV LED wash pars (las luces de lavado / los pares)
- 2x SHEHDS 36x18W RGBWA+UV zoom moving wash heads (las cabezas móviles)
- 2x Chauvet Swarm Wash FX (efectos de luces / los Swarms)
- Rockforce 384 DMX controller (la consola de luces)
- 2x 13' crank stands with T-bars (los tripies de manivela)
- 1x 9' light stand with T-bar

## Layout

- Crank stands stage left and right, T-bars parallel to stage edges
- Each crank stand: 2 wash pars + 1 moving wash head
- 9' stand FOH with the 2 Swarm FX units
- 2 remaining wash pars as floor uplights behind the band (drums get UV/amber)
- No follow spot — one moving wash doubles as drag spot

## DMX Addressing

- 16-channel increments, one fixture per scanner button (10 fixtures total)
- Scanners 1–6: wash pars (10CH mode, addresses 001–081)
- Scanners 7–8: moving washes (12CH mode, addresses 097, 113)
- Scanners 9–10: Swarm Wash FX (2CH mode, addresses 129, 131)

- la dirección DMX — the DMX address
- la prueba de sonido — the soundcheck
