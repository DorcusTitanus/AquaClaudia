## Core Idea (captured March 15, 2026)
Our brains already run a simulation of reality. Optical illusions, inattentional blindness, and constructed experience prove the gap between model and reality.

## The Tautology
We are both the creators of the simulation and the standard by which we measure its fidelity. Circular by design — can't step outside our own model.

## The Payoff
Because we *are* the simulation, we have control surfaces for experience. Reprogramming perception isn't deception — it's changing the interface.

**Maps to:** neuroplasticity, CBT (cognitive restructuring), IFS, deliberate practice/method acting, growth mindset (Dweck)

## Research Directions
- Predictive processing / active inference (Karl Friston)
- Embodied cognition

- Perception as construction (Anil Seth — strongest anchor, "controlled hallucination" framework)
- Seth + Friston together = academic foundation; CBT/IFS/neuroplasticity = where it becomes Jefe's own
