## The Project

SAC Health running org-wide strategy launch for 800+ employees. Two simultaneous tracks on July 29:

- **Track A — Flight Deck:** Full-day leadership summit for 100+ managers/executives at Norton Regional Events Center, San Bernardino. Dave owns entirely.
- **Track B — Flight Path:** Branded immersive experience for 800+ employees at SAC Health Brier 5th Floor Open Space, 1003 E Brier Drive, San Bernardino, CA 92408. Jeff owns technology and ID.

**Core concept:** Employees are pilots in training. Every station = a training milestone. Destination = SBD International. Culminates in earning wings.

**Primary deliverable:** 85–90% of 800 employees submitting individual cascading OKRs into Lattice by end of August 2026. First-ever org-wide OKR rollout for SAC Health.
